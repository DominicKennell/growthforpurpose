This work is personal to me

I've lived the experience of struggling with routine, consistency, and sticking to a training programme — even when I knew what I should be doing.

I understand how easy it is for motivation to dip, for life to take over, and for confidence to falter.

That lived experience informs how I coach. I don't provide fitness or nutrition plans. Instead, I work alongside Personal Trainers and nutrition professionals to support the behavioural and mindset side of change — helping women stay accountable, build confidence, and follow through.
